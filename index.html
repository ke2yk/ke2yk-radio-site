Welcome To The KE2YK Ham Radio Site

I have been a ham radio operator since 1990. I enjoy working the HF bands. CW is my favorite mode. I can copy and send at 25 WPM. Another aspect of the hobby I enjoy is building kits and experimenting with HF antennas. To find out more about Ham Radio visit my Ham Radio Blog. 73 de KE2YK.
